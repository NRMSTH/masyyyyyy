Background

Who Is He?

Azroy Shaf bin Alias

Harum Manis Garderner, Perlis

Educational & Career Background

After completing his Sijil Pelajaran Malaysia (SPM), Azroy continued his studies in rubber handling skills. He was then introduced by his friends to the field of agriculture, specifically paddy and Harum Manis cultivation. From that exposure, he decided to venture into agriculture and continued to develop his skills and experience in this sector.

How to get involved with Harum Manis?

Azroy began his involvement in the Harum Manis cultivation sector when he followed in the footsteps of his friends who had previously ventured into the field. Through direct experience and learning from experienced individuals, he acquired the knowledge and skills he needed. As a result, he developed an interest and confidence to seriously engage in the Harum Manis cultivation industry.

Experience in this Harum Manis agricultural field?

With experience in paddy cultivation and 16 years of involvement in Harum Manis cultivation, Azroy ventured into this field not only out of interest, but also driven by high demand from the public. This decision was also influenced by his move from Penang to Perlis in 2010, which opened up wider opportunities for him to grow in the agricultural industry, especially in the production of the famous Harum Manis in the state.

💡

Azroy is now actively managing and working on a farm located in Kangar, Perlis. This transition reflects his adaptability and continued growth in the ever-expanding agricultural industry.

Challenges

The Biggest Challenge Throughout The Job

☀️🌧️

Unstable Weather

Extreme hot weather causes stress on trees, while the monsoon season around October increases the risk of fungal attacks on fruit to more than four spots on a single fruit.

🧪

Pesticide Management

An incorrect or excessive mixture of pesticides can have a negative effect on trees — causing the tree to produce many flowers but not fruit well.

🌸🥭

Flowering and Fruiting Season

The biggest challenge is during the flowering and fruiting season — this is when care needs to be more careful because any mistakes can have a major impact on the harvest.
