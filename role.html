Job & Role

Azroy's daily work and contributions to the Perlis Harum Manis industry

Daily tasks

Daily Routines

01
💊

Foliar Spray / Fertilizer

After the flowering season, Azroy sprays the trees with foliar pesticides or fertilizers at least once a week to ensure the health of the trees is always maintained and protected from disease attacks.

02
🔍

Farm Monitoring

The plantation is monitored regularly every three days to identify any signs of disease or problems in the trees. Early monitoring allows immediate action to be taken before the problem spreads more widely.

03
✂️

Tree Pruning

Prune unproductive buds and branches regularly to ensure ventilation and sunlight can penetrate the interior of the tree better, thus improving the quality of fruit.

04
🌸

Young Fruit Control

Some young fruits are strategically removed to reduce the burden on the tree and allow the remaining fruits to grow more fully and achieve higher quality.

Care Techniques

Main Role in the Production of Harum Manis

Azroy takes care of the Harum Manis tree by implementing several pruning and treatment techniques to ensure the quality of the fruit produced is better. Each technique is carried out carefully based on his 16 years of experience in this field.

🌱

Vegetative Growth Control

Prune the tree's top three times to control vegetative growth so that the tree's energy is more focused on fruit production. This technique ensures that the tree does not waste energy on unnecessary leaf and branch growth.

🔬

Hormone Use

Schedule the use of hormones on the trunk of Harum Manis trees to stimulate the process of fruit formation and growth optimally. The use of the right hormones at the right time is the key to producing quality fruit.

🌤️

Light Management & Ventilation

Prune some unproductive tree branches to ensure proper ventilation and sunlight can penetrate the interior of the tree. Good ventilation also helps reduce the risk of fungal attacks and diseases.

🍋

Fruit Quality Control

Remove some young fruits to reduce the tree's burden and allow the remaining fruits to grow more fully and to a higher quality. This step ensures that each fruit receives sufficient nutrition to achieve the best size and quality.

Importance to Perlis

Contribution to Perlis

Azroy explained that Harum Manis fruit has great importance to the state of Perlis, especially in terms of branding as a unique and high quality local fruit. Demand for this fruit continues to increase, including for export purposes abroad.

🏷️

State Brand

Harum Manis has become the identity and pride of the State of Perlis as a unique local fruit with high quality at the national level.

📈

Demand is Increasing

The demand for this fruit is increasing over time, showing consumer confidence in the quality of Harum Manis Perlis.

✈️

International Export

Harum Manis is now exported abroad including Japan, Singapore and several other countries, strengthening its position as a high-value agricultural product from Perlis.

🌍

Global Recognition

Through this export, the name Harum Manis Perlis is increasingly known internationally, bringing fame to the state and country of Malaysia.
